## üß† Core Frameworks

### The Triage Pillars
The system triages user needs into three fundamental dimensions of existence:
*   **TIME**: Chronobiology, circadian rhythm alignment, and ultradian cycles. *Are you fighting your biological clock?*
*   **SPACE**: Physical health, fascial integrity, and environmental design. *Is your vessel and environment supporting you?*
*   **SELF**: Identity, emotional processing, and cognitive load. *Is your internal software calibrated?*

### The Assessment Trinity (IQ/EQ/KQ)
Unlike traditional wellness apps that track only steps or calories, we track the three intelligences:
*   **IQ (Intellectual Quotient)**: Focus duration, mental clarity, and executive function.
*   **EQ (Emotional Quotient)**: Emotional regulation, relationship quality, and stress resilience.
*   **KQ (Kinesthetic Quotient)**: Interoception (internal body sense), proprioception (movement sense), and physical intuition.
